Welcome to the world of Teyvat, a breathtaking land brimming with elemental energy, ancient mysteries, and unforgettable adventures.
In Genshin Impact, you embark on a journey as the Traveler, searching for your lost sibling while unraveling the secrets of the Seven Nations.
Along the way, you’ll meet a diverse cast of characters, each with unique personalities, elemental abilities, and stories to share.
Master the art of elemental combat by combining powers such as Anemo, Geo, Electro, and more to overcome formidable foes and solve intricate
puzzles. Discover hidden treasures, challenge powerful bosses, and participate in dynamic events that bring the world of Teyvat to life.

What is GENSHIN IMPACT

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game developed and
published by miHoYo. Set in the fantasy world of Teyvat, players take on the
role of the Traveler, who is searching for their lost sibling while unraveling
the mysteries of the Seven Nations and their respective Archons. The game
features a vast open world filled with diverse landscapes, dungeons, and
secrets to discover. Players can explore the world, engage in real-time combat
using a variety of characters with unique elemental abilities, and complete
quests to progress the story. Genshin Impact also includes a gacha system for
acquiring new characters and weapons, as well as multiplayer co-op gameplay.
Since its release in September 2020, Genshin Impact has gained widespread
popularity for its stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and rich lore.

Genshin Impact is available on multiple platforms, including PC, PlayStation 4,
PlayStation 5, iOS, and Android. The game supports cross-platform play,
allowing players to team up with friends regardless of their chosen device.
Genshin Impact is free-to-play, with in-game purchases available through
a gacha system that allows players to obtain new characters and weapons.
The game receives regular updates and expansions, introducing new content
such as characters, regions, events, and storylines to keep the experience
fresh and engaging for players.

As of 2024, Genshin Impact has introduced several regions, each with its own
unique culture, environment, and elemental affinity. The known regions include
Mondstadt (Anemo), Liyue (Geo), Inazuma (Electro), Sumeru (Dendro), Fontaine
(Hydro), Natlan (Pyro), and the upcoming regions of Nod-Krai and Snezhnaya.
Each region is governed by a different Archon, who plays a significant role in the
game's lore and storyline. Players can explore these regions, uncover their secrets,
and interact with the diverse cast of characters that inhabit them.

REGIONS

MONDSTADT

Mondstadt is a vibrant, wind-swept city known
for its freedom, towering churches,
and lush landscapes, all protected
by the Anemo Archon, Barbatos.

LIYUE

Liyue is a bustling, ancient city built upon
commerce and tradition, steeped in the power
of the Geo Archon, Morax, and surrounded
by majestic mountains and seas.

INAZUMA

Inazuma is a storm-battered archipelago ruled
by the Raiden Shogun, where the pursuit of
eternity clashes with the spirits of freedom
amidst a land of powerful lightning storms.

SUMERU

Sumeru is a vast, lush region of knowledge
and wisdom, governed by the Dendro
Archon Nahida, where scholars and sages
seek to unlock the mysteries of the world.

FONTAINE

Fontaine is a city of justice and law, where
the Hydro Archon Focalors upholds a strict
belief in fairness, set against a backdrop
of vast waterways and grandiose architecture.

NATLAN

Natlan is a fiery, passionate region
where the land itself burns with the spirit of
its people, known for its vibrant culture
and its Pyro Archon Mavuika.
